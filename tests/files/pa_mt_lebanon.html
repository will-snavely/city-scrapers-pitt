Commission Meetings

Covid Update:


Until further notice, meetings will be a "hybrid-style meeting," meaning the public may attend in person or attend online via the Zoom platform.

If you wish to attend in person, in Commission Chambers, 710 Washington Road, and participate in the Citizens Comments portion of the meeting, you must reserve your spot by calling Julie Garrubba at 412-343-3404 or emailing jgarrubba@mtlebanon.org by noon on the Tuesday of the meeting. You must give Julie your name and home address, but you do not need to tell her the topic of your comments. Please indicate if you want to speak during public comment or during a particular public hearing. 

If you wish to attend using the Zoom platform, you will need to register online. Once you have registered, you will be sent the link to the meeting. You also will be able to indicate if you wish to speak during citizens comments or any applicable public hearings.

Meetings

  • 8 p.m.
  • Second and fourth Tuesday of each month (unless noted)
  • Commission Chambers, Mt. Lebanon Municipal Building, 710 Washington Road
  • Special meetings held as needed

Discussion Session Meetings

  • 6:30 p.m., preceding regularly scheduled meetings
  • Agendas are posted on the municipal website and on Cable Channels
  • Meetings are held in Room C
  • Residents may attend, but may not speak
For further information regarding commission meetings, please contact the manager's secretary, Julie Garrubba at (412) 343-3404.
